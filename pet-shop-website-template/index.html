About Us

The main purpose of an Animal Diseases Prevalence Analytics System

would be to provide a comprehensive overview of the prevalence of various animal diseases in Rwanda, based on data collected from various sources such as veterinary clinics, farmers, and slaughterhouses.

This system would enable RAB to make data-driven decisions in their efforts to prevent, control,

and eradicate animal diseases in Rwanda, thereby improving the overall health and productivity of the country's livestock population.

Services

Monitoring and evaluation

Monitoring and evaluation

The system would enable RAB to monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of their disease control and eradication programs, enabling them to make necessary adjustments and improvements.
